Invitation

You are kindly invited to Katerina's 30th birthday celebration. Join us for a picnic lunch by the lake of Revine in northern Italy on Saturday, June 1st. Festivities will begin at midday. Feel free to bring along your favorite food, drinks, and games to share! Accommodation is available for those wishing to stay overnight. We have two houses reserved: one near the lake (ideal for one-night stays) and another higher up in the forest (suitable for multiple-night stays). In case of rain, the forest house will be the location of the picnic. It features a spacious garden, a lovely half-covered outdoor space, and a large dining room for our gathering. Please let me know your accommodation preference and arrival details in advance. On June 2nd, after the celebration, we can explore the surroundings together—whether it's a leisurely walk around the lake, visiting the old town, or enjoying any other activities that we decide on together.

Airplane

If you're flying in, there are two main airports nearby: Treviso Airport and Venice Airport. From Treviso Airport: You can take a local bus from Treviso Airport to Treviso train station. From there, you can catch a train to Vittorio Veneto. The total journey is approximately 1 hour. From Venice Airport (Venezia Airport): Take a local bus from Venice Airport to Venezia Mestre train station. From Venezia Mestre, you can board a train to Vittorio Veneto. The total journey is approximately 1 hour and a half. Upon reaching Vittorio Veneto, arrangements will be made for someone to pick you up and drive you to your accomodation. Please inform us of your arrival details for coordination. If you need further details about bus schedules, train connections, or information on booking tickets, please feel free to reach out to me!
